Voidlands

A complete original browser voxel survival game.

Programming, procedural art direction, Pixel Art+ voxel textures, UI, models, terrain systems and synthesized sound were created specifically for this project.

Rendering powered by Three.js. Friend-code WebRTC connections powered by the bundled PeerJS library.

Design principles

Responsive first-person controls, deterministic worlds, reliable collision, local saves and original assets.
